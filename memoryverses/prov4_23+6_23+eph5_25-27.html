Prov 4:23 & 6:23 & Eph 5:25-27

Proverbs 4:23 → Keep thy heart with all diligence; for out of it are the issues of life.

6:23 → For the commandment is a lamp; and the law is light; and reproofs of instruction are the way of life:

Ephesians 5:25-27 → 25 For Husbands, love your wives, even as Christ also loved the church, and gave himself for it; 26 That he might sanctify and cleanse it with the washing of water by the word, 27 That he might present it to himself a glorious church, not having spot, or wrinkle, or any such thing; but that it should be holy and without blemish.
